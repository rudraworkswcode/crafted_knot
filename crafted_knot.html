Our Premium Woods

Oak

Sourced primarily from sustainable forests in Europe and North America, Oak is a robust hardwood celebrated for its strength and distinctive grain patterns. Its pale, golden-brown hue and resistance to warping make it an ideal choice for furniture that is intended to last for generations, offering both durability and a timeless aesthetic.

Teak

Indigenous to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and India, Teak is a highly prized hardwood known for its exceptional durability and natural water resistance. Rich in natural oils, this golden-brown timber requires minimal maintenance and is impervious to dry rot, making it the superior choice for pieces that demand resilience and longevity.

Pine

Harvested from the vast coniferous forests of the Northern Hemisphere, Pine is a lightweight softwood characterised by its pale yellow colour and distinctive dark knots. While more affordable than hardwoods, it offers a charming, rustic appeal and takes stains exceptionally well, making it a versatile option for budget-conscious yet stylish projects.

Rosewood

Sourced from the tropical forests of India and South America, Rosewood is a premium hardwood renowned for its density and deep, reddish-brown hue streaked with darker veins. Its ability to take a high polish and its rich, complex aroma make it a luxury material, often reserved for high-end bespoke furniture and intricate joinery.

Exquisite Finishes

Matte Oil

Our Matte Oil finish is designed to penetrate deep into the timber, nourishing the fibres while maintaining the wood's natural texture and appearance. It provides a low-sheen look that enhances the grain without creating an artificial film, perfect for those who appreciate the organic feel of raw wood.

Satin Varnish

The Satin Varnish offers a balance between protection and aesthetics, creating a durable surface with a soft, subtle sheen. It forms a protective layer against spills and scratches while reflecting a gentle amount of light, making it a practical and elegant choice for dining tables and high-traffic surfaces.

High Gloss

For a contemporary and striking statement, our High Gloss finish creates a glass-like, reflective surface that accentuates the depth of the wood's colour. This multi-layered application requires precision craftsmanship and results in a sleek, modern look that is incredibly smooth to the touch.

Raw / Unfinished

Leaving the piece Raw or Unfinished preserves the wood in its most natural state, allowing the pale tones and texture to take centre stage. This option is ideal for customers who wish to apply their own specific treatment later or for those who desire the authentic, untreated character of the timber to develop a patina over time.

Our Story

From a modest workshop to a hallmark of artisanal excellence.

The Human Element

Over the last six years, Sri Sai Wood Carving Works has evolved from a small local unit into a hub of artisanal excellence. Our journey is defined not just by the wood we shape, but by the hands that shape it.

We believe in transparently celebrating our process. Behind every finished piece are our master artisans—hands often covered in sawdust, wielding chisels with focused intensity and a dedication that machines cannot replicate. We are not a faceless entity, but a team of craftsmen deeply committed to the integrity of our trade.

Precision & Tradition

While we honour tradition, we also embrace the future. Our workshop represents a duality of workflow where time-honoured skills meet modern technology.

Alongside our manual tools, we operate advanced CNC machinery. This juxtaposition allows us to achieve unparalleled precision where it counts, ensuring structural integrity without losing the soul of the handcrafted. It is this balance of the human touch and mechanical accuracy that builds trust and delivers furniture of lasting value.
